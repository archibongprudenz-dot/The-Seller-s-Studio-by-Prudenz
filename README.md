# SellerStudio – by Prudenz

SellerStudio is a simple digital marketing support tool designed to help entrepreneurs promote their products professionally and attract more customers through quick visual content creation.

## Features

* **Complimentary Card Creator:** Design clean and simple business or appreciation cards for customers and brand promotion.
* **WhatsApp Product Boost:** Create ready-to-share promotional product visuals optimized for WhatsApp marketing.
* **Smart Promotion Badges:** Add attention-grabbing labels such as *Discount*, *New Arrival*, and *Special Offer*.
* **Automatic Price Highlighting:** Display previous prices with strike-through formatting and clearly present new promotional prices.
* **Fast Visual Branding:** Helps sellers create neat, attractive product promotion graphics without design stress.

## Purpose

SellerStudio is built to help small business owners improve product visibility, communicate offers clearly, and market their goods more confidently using simple digital tools.
